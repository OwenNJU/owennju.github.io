About me

I am a final-year PhD candidate at School of Computer Science, Nanjing University, under the supervision of Prof. Shujian Huang and Prof. Jiajun Chen. Starting soon, I will join Bytedance Seed as a researcher.

Previously, I had the privilege to research multilingual large language models and machine translation at The University of Edinburgh (UoE) and Shanghai AI Lab (SH-Lab). I am deeply grateful for the opportunity to work with and learn from Prof. Alexandra Birch (UoE), Dr. Jingjing Xu (SH-Lab), Prof. Lei Li (CMU), and Prof. Lingpeng Kong (HKU). Their guidance and support have been invaluable to my academic development.

Feel free to reach out to me, I’d be happy to connect!
